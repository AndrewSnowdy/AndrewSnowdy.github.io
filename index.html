About Me

Welcome to my portfolio. I am an electrical engineer with a deep focus on controls and automation. My passion lies in designing and implementing robust systems that solve complex challenges, from microgrid power restoration to autonomous vehicle control.

Throughout my academic and personal projects, I've developed a strong skill set in power electronics, embedded systems, and control theory. I enjoy the entire engineering process, from initial concept and simulation in tools like MATLAB/Simulink to hands-on prototyping, soldering, and testing.

This website showcases a selection of my work. Please feel free to explore my projects and get in touch if you'd like to connect.
